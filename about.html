Here at GAIA we want to spread awareness of these very dangerous and threatings issues ecosystems are going under,

what is happening to these ecosystems we both love and require to maintain our lives & world, we should aid these

With our word being spread, we hope that it is able to reach not only other fellow companies who may not be involved
in these types of stuff, but also governments across the world to take action against these threats! however,
we also wanna let the general public know about these problems so they not only spread our word but also be able
to realise that they have the problem to help stop these threats through their own actions alone!


Besides spreading the word of these threats getting to a range of different people, we also offer all sorts of
Soluations to these problems that will help either lessen it's impact upon the ecosystems we wish to protect or
it removes it entirely to avoid it ever being a threat again to these places!
